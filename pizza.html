Pizza

This is a delicious recipe for pizza.

This homemade pizza is a crowd-pleaser with its crispy crust, flavorful tomato sauce, and gooey cheese. Customize it with your favorite toppings for a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olives
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Oregano
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni slices, olives , bell peppers, and onions.
  6. Season with oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top of the pizza.
  8. Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
