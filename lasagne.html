Lasagne

This is a delicious recipe for lasagne.

This hearty spinach lasagna is the best dinner choice for cheese lovers as it's filled with Romano, ricotta, and mozzarella cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne sheets
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Spinach
  • Romano cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil
  • Oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, spinach, salt and pepper to the skillet. Cook until the spinach is wilted and the tomato mixture is heated through.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta, Romano, and mozzarella cheeses.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the tomato mixture. Layer lasagne sheets on top, followed by more tomato mixture and a layer of the cheese mixture. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  6. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let the lasagne cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil and oregano if desired.
